Steve Teoh: Bibliography
Dr Steve (—Chee Hooi) Teoh is a Senior Lecturer (Ts Dr) in Computer Science at Taylor’s University, Subang Jaya, within the Faculty of Innovation & Technology.
He holds a PhD in Engineering from UTAR, along with degrees from University of Essex (MSc CS) and Campbell University (B.S. CS).

Research Areas
His focus areas include ICT infrastructure, wireless devices, and digital health.

Job Title
He’s also the Hub Leader for Digital Healthcare & Data Analytics at Taylor’s Impact Labs, which works on innovative solutions in diagnostics, disease prevention, and healthcare accessibility.
He is an Enterprise Director and inventor in the tech sector, he blends research with entrepreneurial insight.
